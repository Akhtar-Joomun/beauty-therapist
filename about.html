Bibi Af's career path

Bibi Af is a highly experienced mobile beauty therapist who has over 20 years of experience, working in the industry. She obtained her diploma in 1998.

Bibi Af is a highly experienced mobile beauty therapist who has over 20 years of experience, working in the industry. She obtained her diploma in 1998. Her career started in a Beauty salon in Rose-Hill where she was a beauty therapist for four years. Bibi Af then opened her own salon in Grand Bay. She worked for a few more years and then decided to switch to home service. Her customers are diversed with the majority of foreigners and expats. In 2013 she left Mauritius to travel and work as a beauty therapist in Paris for a year followed by 3 more years in London. Bibi Af returned to Mauritius in 2017 to occupy the same position of mobile beauty therapist... with more experience and now with a team of 3 people.
